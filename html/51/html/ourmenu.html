Shushi

Delicious fresh sushi made with fresh fish

  • Nigiri

    • A small hand-pressed mound of rice topped with fish or seafood, such as salmon or tuna.

  • Maki

    • Rice and fillings rolled in seaweed and sliced into pieces. Examples include cucumber rolls and tuna rolls.

  • Uramaki

    • An inside-out roll where the rice is on the outside and the seaweed is on the inside. The California Roll is a famous example.

  • Temaki

    • A hand roll shaped like a cone of seaweed filled with rice, fish, and vegetables.

  • Sashimi

    • Thin slices of raw fish served without rice. While technically not sushi (because it contains no sushi rice), it is commonly served alongside sushi.
